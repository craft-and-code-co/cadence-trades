# Maintenance Plan Structures — What to Include by Trade

A maintenance membership program is the most important recurring revenue asset your trades business can build. It creates predictable income, fills your shoulder season schedule, improves customer retention, and gives your techs guaranteed calls when demand is slow. Companies with 1,000+ active memberships typically have 20-40% of their annual revenue locked in before January 1st.

## HVAC Maintenance Plans

HVAC is the easiest trade to sell memberships because homeowners understand that their AC and furnace need regular maintenance. Most manufacturers require annual maintenance to keep warranty coverage valid — that's a powerful selling point.

### What to Include

**Standard Plan (1 visit per year):**
- AC tune-up OR furnace tune-up (seasonal)
- Full system inspection (electrical connections, refrigerant levels, airflow, safety controls)
- Filter replacement (standard 1" filter)
- Written report of system condition
- Priority scheduling (next-day service guarantee)
- 15% discount on repairs

**Premium Plan (2 visits per year):**
- Everything in Standard, plus:
- Both AC tune-up AND furnace tune-up (spring and fall)
- Condensate drain treatment
- Thermostat calibration
- No overtime charges for after-hours emergency calls
- 20% discount on repairs
- 10% discount on equipment replacement

**Elite Plan (2 visits + extras):**
- Everything in Premium, plus:
- Indoor air quality check
- Duct inspection
- Filter delivered to home quarterly (upgraded filter)
- Waived diagnostic fee ($89-149 value)
- Front-of-the-line scheduling
- 25% discount on repairs

## Plumbing Maintenance Plans

Plumbing memberships are harder to sell because homeowners don't think about plumbing until something goes wrong. Frame it as protection: "Your plumbing system has 100+ connection points. One failure can cause $10,000 in water damage. Our plan catches problems before they become emergencies."

### What to Include

**Standard Plan (1 visit per year):**
- Whole-home plumbing inspection (water heater, supply lines, drains, fixtures, hose bibs)
- Water heater flush and anode rod inspection
- Water pressure test
- Check for leaks at all visible connections
- Priority scheduling
- 15% discount on repairs

**Premium Plan (2 visits per year):**
- Everything in Standard, plus:
- Second inspection (spring and fall — checking seasonal items like hose bibs, sump pump)
- Drain camera inspection of main sewer line
- Water quality test
- No overtime charges
- 20% discount on repairs
- Waived service call fee

## Electrical Maintenance Plans

Electrical memberships are the hardest sell but incredibly valuable once established. Position around safety: "Electrical fires cause $1.3 billion in property damage annually. Our inspection catches the problems that cause fires."

### What to Include

**Standard Plan (1 visit per year):**
- Whole-home electrical safety inspection
- Panel inspection and thermal scan
- GFCI/AFCI outlet testing
- Smoke and CO detector testing
- Surge protection check
- Written safety report
- Priority scheduling
- 15% discount on repairs

**Premium Plan (includes generator customers):**
- Everything in Standard, plus:
- Generator load test and maintenance (for generator customers)
- Whole-home surge protector inspection
- Outlet and switch tightening
- 20% discount on repairs and installations
- Waived diagnostic fee

## Backflow Maintenance Plans

Backflow is the easiest membership to sell because testing is legally required annually. Your membership is essentially "We'll handle your compliance so you never get fined."

### What to Include

**Annual Backflow Plan:**
- Annual backflow preventer test (required by municipality)
- Certification filing with the water authority
- Minor repairs included (gaskets, seals under $50 in parts)
- Reminder notifications (so the customer never misses a deadline)
- Priority scheduling during testing season
- 15% discount on backflow repairs or replacements

## Multi-Trade Bundled Plans

If you offer multiple trades, bundle them for a premium price and massive customer lock-in.

**Whole-Home Protection Plan:**
- HVAC tune-ups (2x/year)
- Plumbing inspection + water heater flush (1x/year)
- Electrical safety inspection (1x/year)
- All discounts and priority scheduling from individual plans
- One annual price, one relationship, total peace of mind

Price this 10-15% below the sum of individual plans. The perceived savings drive sign-ups, and the multi-touch relationship makes these customers nearly impossible for competitors to steal.

## Plan Naming

Don't call them "Basic/Standard/Premium." Use names that convey value:
- **Silver / Gold / Platinum**
- **Shield / Guard / Fortress**
- **Essential / Preferred / Ultimate**
- **Partner / Preferred Partner / VIP**

The name should make the customer feel like they're getting something exclusive, not buying the cheapest option.
