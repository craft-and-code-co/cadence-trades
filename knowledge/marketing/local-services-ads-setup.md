# Google Local Services Ads (LSA) — Setup, Verification, and Optimization

Google Local Services Ads are the single best lead source for most trades companies. They appear above everything else in Google search results — above regular search ads, above the map pack, at the very top of the page. And you only pay when someone actually contacts you through the ad, not when they click. For trades companies, LSAs typically generate leads at $15-45 each, which is 30-50% cheaper than regular Google Search ads.

## How LSAs Work

When a homeowner searches "plumber near me" or "AC repair [city]," your LSA appears at the top with:
- Your company name
- Google review rating and count
- Years in business
- Hours of operation
- "Google Guaranteed" or "Google Screened" badge

The homeowner can call you directly from the ad or send a message. You pay per lead (call or message), not per click. Typical lead costs:
- HVAC: $25-45 per lead
- Plumbing: $20-40 per lead
- Electrical: $20-35 per lead
- Backflow/other: $15-30 per lead

## Setup Process

### Step 1: Create Your Profile

Go to ads.google.com/local-services-ads and create your business profile. You'll need:
- Business name, address, phone number
- Service areas (zip codes or radius)
- Business hours
- Services you offer (select from Google's list)
- Year founded

### Step 2: Background Check and Verification

This is the part that takes time (2-6 weeks) but is also what makes LSAs valuable — the verification barrier keeps out fly-by-night operators.

**Required for Google Guarantee:**
- **Business license verification:** Upload your state/local business license
- **Insurance verification:** General liability insurance (Google has minimum requirements — typically $500K-1M)
- **Background checks:** Owner and all field employees must pass background checks through Google's partner (Pinkerton or similar)
- **Trade license:** State contractor's license, journeyman license, or master license depending on your trade and state

**Tips to speed up verification:**
- Have all documents ready before you start
- Make sure your insurance certificate lists the exact business name you registered
- Respond to any verification requests within 24 hours
- If background checks stall, contact Google support — sometimes they need a nudge

### Step 3: Set Your Budget

LSA budgets work as a weekly maximum. Google will try to spend up to that amount but may vary day to day.

**Starting budgets:**
- Small market (under 200K population): $100-200/week
- Medium market (200K-1M): $200-400/week
- Large market (1M+): $400-800/week

You can always adjust. Start lower and increase as you confirm the leads are quality.

### Step 4: Select Your Services

Be specific about which services you want to advertise. You can turn services on and off at any time.

**HVAC selections:** AC repair, AC installation, heating repair, heating installation, duct cleaning, maintenance
**Plumbing selections:** Drain cleaning, water heater repair, water heater installation, leak repair, faucet repair, sewer service
**Electrical selections:** Outlet repair, panel upgrade, wiring, lighting installation, generator installation

**Pro tip:** Start with your highest-margin services only. If water heater replacements are your best money-maker, lead with that. Add lower-margin services later as you build review volume.

## Optimization Strategies

### Reviews Are Everything

Your ranking in LSAs is heavily influenced by your Google review count and rating. A company with 350 reviews at 4.8 stars will appear above a company with 40 reviews at 5.0 stars.

**Review generation tactics:**
- Ask every satisfied customer for a review before your tech leaves the job
- Send an automated review request via text/email within 2 hours of job completion
- Make it easy — send a direct link to your Google review page
- Respond to every review (positive and negative) within 24 hours
- Target: 10-20 new reviews per month minimum

### Responsiveness Score

Google tracks how quickly you respond to LSA leads. Fast response = higher ranking.

- **Answer calls within 3 rings** or have them roll to a live answering service
- **Respond to messages within 5 minutes** during business hours
- **Never let a lead sit overnight** — your responsiveness score will tank

### Dispute Bad Leads

You can dispute leads that aren't legitimate:
- Wrong service area
- Spam or solicitation calls
- Services you don't offer
- Duplicate leads from the same customer

Dispute within 30 days. Google credits disputed leads back to your account. This keeps your actual cost per real lead lower. Check your leads weekly and dispute anything that doesn't qualify.

### Booking Rate

Google also tracks what percentage of leads you actually book. A higher booking rate signals to Google that you're providing a good customer experience, which improves your ranking.

- Train your CSRs to convert LSA calls (these leads are hot — 40-60% should book)
- Don't quote prices over the phone if you can avoid it — book the diagnostic call
- Follow up on missed calls within 30 minutes

## The Bottom Line

LSAs should be your first marketing investment. They're the easiest, cheapest, highest-quality lead source in trades marketing. Get verified, build your reviews, answer fast, and the leads will flow. Most trades companies see LSAs become their #1 lead source within 6 months of launching.
